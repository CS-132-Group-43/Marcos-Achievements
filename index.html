Here's an overview of our project.

It started with a question and ended with a period.

Motivation

Understand and combat the rampant misinformation that played a large part in the election campaigns of 2022.

Research Question

Did BBM's announcement of his intent to run and/or the day he filed his certificate of candidacy have something to do with the influx of tweets regarding his credentials?

Null Hypothesis

BBM's announcement of his intent to run and/or the day he filed his certificate of candidacy did not see an increase in the number of tweets defending his credentials.

Action Plan

Analyze tweets about Marcos' credentials/achievements and note how much of these were posted relatively near the two events specified.

Let's take a look at our data

We mined the internet for tweets related to Marcos's achievements that contain disinformation or misinformation.

The data were scraped from March 23 - 24, 2023. We managed to scrape over 2000 tweets, These tweets contain the keywords "bbm", "oxford", "wharton" and are from 2021 to 2022. However, only 115 tweets made it to our primary dataset since majority of these tweets were irrelevant to our topic or did not contain [mis/dis]information. The following features were noted:

  • The tweet's content
  • The tweet's posting period
  • The tweet's rating according to the researchers, with the classification:
    • Rational: informational, functional, educational, or current event
    • Emotional: interactional, experiential, personal, community, relationship, cause-related
    • Transactional: product/service ads, branding, sales promotion, monetary
  • The tweet's metadata such as Likes, Replies, Retweets, and Quote Tweets

After obtaining our data, we explored and played around with it.

Features with missing values were detected by applying isnull() to a copy of the dataset then getting the sum per relevant column. The missing values were then manually filled in. Our data didn't have any outliers so we did not need to handle them. The data were manually formatted in accordance with the project specifications. Since our project involved time-series analysis, we performed interpolation and binning on the data.
